What is the building permit fee for a $150,000 shed?
For a Class 10A shed valued at $150,000, the permit fee is $1,270 plus a lodgement fee of $138.43 and inspection fees. If your property is within a Heritage Overlay, planning approval is required before a building permit can be issued.
What are the fees for registering a dog?
Desexed dog: $50 (pensioner $25). Not desexed: $228 (pensioner $114). Your dog must be microchipped and at least 3 months old. Register online at casey.vic.gov.au — tags arrive within 5–10 working days.
When is my bin collection day in Oran Park?
Your bin collection day in Oran Park is Wednesday. Put your bins out Tuesday evening. Red (garbage), yellow (recycling), and green (garden organics) are all collected weekly. Lids must be fully closed.
Do I need a permit to remove a tree on the Surf Coast?
It depends on the tree and your zone. Properties within a Significant Landscape Overlay or Vegetation Protection Overlay require a planning permit to remove most trees. Some trees under 5 metres may be exempt. Can you confirm your property address or zone?
What are the rules for keeping livestock?
In residential areas you need a permit from Council — the fee is $106 (lifetime of the animals). On rural or farming-zoned land, livestock is generally permitted under the planning scheme without a local law permit.
How do I apply for a building permit in Kilmore?
A building permit is required for new buildings, additions, garages, sheds, decks, retaining walls, pools, and demolition. Contact the Planning and Building Office on 03 5734 6200 for pre-application advice.
How do I apply for a development application?
The DA process has 7 stages. Start by checking if you actually need one — some minor works qualify as exempt or complying development. A free Duty Officer consultation is strongly recommended before preparing anything.
What are the bin collection days for Torquay?
In Torquay, general waste is collected weekly, recycling fortnightly, and FOGO weekly. Your specific collection day depends on your street. I can help you find your ward collector contact or guide you to the Surf Coast waste portal.
How do I apply for a development application in The Hills Shire?
Not all work requires a full DA — some qualifies as exempt or complying development. If you do need a DA, submit online via the application form with your plans and fee (from $385 for works up to $10k). Track progress via the DA Tracking System.
When is my bin collection day in Shellharbour?
Bin days vary by street and suburb. The quickest way is to look up your address on the Find my bin day tool at shellharbourwaste.com.au. You can also call the waste services team (REMONDIS) on 1300 121 344, Monday to Friday 8:30am–5:00pm.
What are the bin collection days in Bright?
Bright is an urban area so FOGO (green lid) is collected every week. Recycling (yellow lid) and general waste (red lid) alternate fortnightly. Week 1: FOGO + Recycling. Week 2: FOGO + General Waste. Check your rates notice or call 03 5755 0555.
What community services are available in Warragul?
Warragul offers the Civic Centre at 210 Main Street for rates, planning, and permits. The Warragul Library has free membership and WiFi. There's also the Aquatic Centre, maternal and child health clinics, and immunisation programs. Phone (03) 5624 2411.
How do I book foreshore camping on the Mornington Peninsula?
You can book foreshore camping at Rosebud, Rye, or Sorrento online through the Mornington Peninsula Shire website. Or call 5950 1060 to book over the phone. Sites have specific rules on pets, fees, and facilities.

Analytics & Data

Every interaction becomes intelligence

CouncilGenius includes a built-in analytics and feedback system that gives council a clear picture of what its community actually wants to know — without collecting any personal information.

Every resident query is automatically classified into one of 22+ service categories. The category label and timestamp are written to a secure log — no personal data, no IP addresses, no message content.

Resident feedback — a simple helpfulness rating per response — is captured alongside category data. Council can download the full analytics and feedback logs at any time via secure administrator endpoints.

This enables council to identify peak enquiry types, detect seasonal patterns, prioritise website improvements, and benchmark service demand year-on-year — from a single, privacy-compliant data source.

Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)

No resident personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted. The analytics system records only anonymised category labels and timestamps — consistent with how councils use standard web analytics platforms.

Privacy & Data Protection Act 2014 (Vic)

Designed in accordance with OVIC best practice guidance on de-identified analytics. No data collected by CouncilGenius constitutes personal information under either Act.

Data returned to council

Analytics and feedback data are the council's own asset. Downloaded at any time via secure administrator endpoints. No third-party access. No data sold or shared.
